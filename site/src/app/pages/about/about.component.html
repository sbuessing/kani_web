About Kani Reader

Wanikani kanji and word definitions are used, and use is covered by www.wanikani.com/terms.
Kuromoji and Kuromojin are used for segmentation.
Rikaikun has a good API for J-E dictionary access.
JMdict is used for non-Wanikani word definitions.
JLPT word and kanji lists are from www.tanos.co.uk/jlpt
Tatoeba sentences are used.
Translations are from Google Translate, and not always useful.
